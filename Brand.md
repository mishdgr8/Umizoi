HERO STATEMENT

UMIZOI 海沿い
Where the sea chooses the course.

Twelve seats.
One tide.
No menu.

ORIGIN STORY

There are restaurants that serve fish.
And there are restaurants that listen to the sea.

UMIZOI was not conceived from a business plan.
It was shaped by tide charts, morning auctions, and the discipline of Edomae tradition.

Each day begins before light —
not in a kitchen,
but at the docks.

The sea decides what will be served.
The chef decides what will be remembered.

PHILOSOPHY — “THE SEA DECIDES.”

We do not design tasting menus weeks in advance.

We follow:

Seasonal migration

Water temperature shifts

Lunar cycles

Auction quality

If the catch is imperfect, we do not serve it.

Omakase, in its truest form, means trust.
At UMIZOI, it also means surrender.

THE EXPERIENCE

Twelve guests.
Seven o’clock.
One synchronized progression.

Hokkaido uni layered like silk.
Bluefin otoro brushed once — never twice.
Scallop cut to alter its sweetness.

Each piece is placed.
Never handed.

Silence is part of the course.

THE CHEF

Precision is not intensity.
It is restraint.

Twenty-three years behind the counter.
Three cities.
One philosophy:

“Remove everything unnecessary.”
